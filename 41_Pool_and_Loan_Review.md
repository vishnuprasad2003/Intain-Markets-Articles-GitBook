# Pool & Loan Review

## Overview

This guide covers how investors and lenders review pools and loans shared with them. Learn how to evaluate investment opportunities, analyze pool characteristics, and make informed decisions.

## Receiving Pool Previews

### How Pools Are Shared

**When pools are shared:**
- Issuer shares pool with you
- Pool appears in your preview queue
- You receive notification
- Pool status is Preview

### What You See

**Pool information:**
- Pool details and description
- Aggregate metrics (balance, WAC, FICO, LTV, DTI/DSCR)
- Loan count and characteristics
- Asset class and transaction type
- Issuer information

---

## Reviewing Pools

### Step 1: Access Pool

**How to access:**
1. Check notifications or preview queue
2. Navigate to shared pools
3. Open pool details
4. Review pool information

### Step 2: Analyze Pool Metrics

**What to evaluate:**
- Total Balance: Pool size and scale
- Weighted Average Coupon (WAC): Interest rate profile
- Average FICO: Credit quality
- Average LTV: Loan-to-value ratios
- Average DTI/DSCR: Debt service capacity
- Loan Count: Number of loans

### Step 3: Review Loan Characteristics

**What to review:**
- Individual loan details
- Loan distribution
- Risk characteristics
- Performance metrics
- Other loan attributes

### Step 4: Evaluate Investment Opportunity

**What to assess:**
- Investment attractiveness
- Risk profile
- Return potential
- Market conditions
- Fit with investment criteria

---

## Reviewing Loans

### Loan-Level Analysis

**What to review:**
- Individual loan details
- Borrower information
- Loan terms and rates
- Collateral information (if applicable)
- Performance data
- Risk metrics

### Loan Quality Assessment

**What to assess:**
- Credit quality
- Loan terms
- Collateral quality (if applicable)

- Risk factors

## Making Decisions

### Option 1: Express Interest

**When to express interest:**
- Pool meets investment criteria
- Interested in opportunity
- Want to proceed
- Ready to invest

**How to express:**
1. Review pool thoroughly
2. Evaluate opportunity
3. Click "Express Interest" or similar
4. Provide contact information
5. Issuer notified

### Option 2: Request More Information

**When to request:**
- Need additional details
- Want clarification
- Have questions
- Need more data

**How to request:**
1. Review pool
2. Identify information needed
3. Click "Request Information" or add comments
4. Specify what you need
5. Issuer responds

### Option 3: Decline Interest

**When to decline:**
- Pool doesn't meet criteria
- Not interested
- Not right fit
- Cannot proceed

**How to decline:**
1. Review pool
2. Decide not interested
3. May provide feedback (optional)
4. Decline opportunity
5. Issuer notified

---

## Best Practices

### For Investors/Lenders

**Review process:**
1. **Review thoroughly**: Analyze pool completely
2. **Evaluate metrics**: Check all key statistics
3. **Assess risk**: Evaluate risk profile
4. **Consider fit**: Assess alignment with criteria
5. **Make informed decision**: Base decision on analysis

**Decision making:**
1. **Be clear**: Communicate decisions clearly
2. **Be timely**: Respond promptly
3. **Be professional**: Maintain good communication
4. **Be specific**: Provide specific feedback if declining

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Immediate Interest

1. Receive pool preview → Review
2. Pool meets criteria → Express interest
3. Issuer contacts → Discussion begins
4. Proceed with investment → Deal progresses

### Scenario 2: Need More Information

1. Receive pool preview → Review
2. Need more details → Request information
3. Issuer provides → Review additional info
4. Make decision → Express interest or decline

### Scenario 3: Not Interested

1. Receive pool preview → Review
2. Doesn't meet criteria → Decline
3. Provide feedback (optional) → Issuer informed
4. Move to next opportunity → Continue reviewing

## Troubleshooting

### "Can't see pool preview"
- Check notifications
- Verify pool was shared with you
- Check preview queue
- Ensure you're logged in as investor

### "Can't access loan details"
- Check pool sharing permissions
- Verify you have view access
- Ensure loans are visible
- Contact issuer if needed
