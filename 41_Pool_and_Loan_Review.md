# Pool and Loan Review

## Overview

This guide covers how investors and lenders review pools and loans shared with them. Learn how to evaluate investment opportunities, analyze pool characteristics, and make informed decisions.

## Who Can Use This

- Investors who review pool opportunities
- Lenders who evaluate loan characteristics

## When This Is Used

Use pool and loan review when:
- Pools are shared with you for investment opportunities
- You need to evaluate investment potential
- You want to analyze pool characteristics
- You're making investment decisions

## Step-by-Step Process

### Reviewing Pools

1. **Access Pool**
   - Check notifications or preview queue
   - Navigate to shared pools
   - Open pool details
   - Review pool information

2. **Analyze Pool Metrics**
   - Review total balance and loan count
   - Evaluate weighted average coupon
   - Assess average FICO scores
   - Check loan-to-value ratios
   - Review debt service metrics

3. **Review Loan Characteristics**
   - View individual loan details
   - Assess loan distribution
   - Evaluate risk characteristics
   - Review performance metrics

4. **Evaluate Investment Opportunity**
   - Assess investment attractiveness
   - Evaluate risk profile
   - Consider return potential
   - Determine fit with your criteria

### Making Decisions

1. **Express Interest**
   - If pool meets your criteria
   - Provide feedback or questions
   - Indicate interest level
   - Request additional information if needed

2. **Download Information** (if permitted)
   - Download pool data if allowed
   - Export loan details
   - Save for further analysis
   - Use for decision making

## Rules & Validations

- You can only see pools shared with you
- Download permissions are controlled by issuer
- Feedback permissions are controlled by issuer
- You can review but cannot modify pools
- Your review doesn't affect pool status

## What Happens Next

After reviewing pools:
- You can express interest or provide feedback
- You can download information if permitted
- Issuer receives your feedback
- You can track pools of interest
- Investment decisions can be made

Understanding pool and loan review helps investors effectively evaluate opportunities and make informed investment decisions.
