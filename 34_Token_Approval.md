# Token Approval

## Overview

Token approval is the step where borrowers approve the transfer of tokens, making funding notices visible to lenders. This guide covers how to review token allocations and approve token transfers.

## Who Can Use This

- Borrowers who have funding notices with generated tokens

## When This Is Used

Use token approval when:
- Tokens have been generated for your funding notice
- You want to proceed with the drawdown
- You need to make funding notice visible to lenders
- You're ready to complete the drawdown process

## Step-by-Step Process

### Reviewing Token Allocation

1. **Access Funding Notice**
   - Navigate to Funding Notices section
   - Find funding notice with tokens generated
   - Open funding notice details
   - Review token allocation

2. **Review Token Details**
   - Check total token amount matches request
   - Verify lender allocations are correct
   - Confirm distribution percentages
   - Review individual lender amounts

3. **Verify Information**
   - Ensure request amount is correct
   - Check funding date is appropriate
   - Verify purpose is accurate
   - Confirm everything looks correct

### Approving Token Transfer

1. **Review Complete**
   - Ensure all information is verified
   - Check token allocation is correct
   - Verify you're ready to proceed

2. **Approve Transfer**
   - Click approve token transfer button
   - Confirm your approval
   - Status updates to show approval

3. **After Approval**
   - Funding notice becomes visible to lenders
   - Lenders can review and approve
   - Drawdown process can proceed
   - You can track lender decisions

## Rules & Validations

- You can only approve tokens after they've been generated
- You must review allocation before approving
- Approval makes funding notice visible to lenders
- Once approved, you cannot undo the approval
- Lenders can then review and approve drawdowns

## What Happens Next

After approving token transfer:
- Funding notice becomes visible to lenders
- Lenders receive notifications
- Lenders can review and approve or reject
- Fund transfer process can begin
- You can track drawdown progress

Understanding token approval helps you complete the drawdown process and enable lenders to participate in your funding requests.
