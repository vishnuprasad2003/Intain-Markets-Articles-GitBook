# Funds Transfer Confirmation

## Overview

Funds transfer confirmation is the final step where lenders confirm that they have transferred funds to the borrower. This completes the drawdown process for each individual lender.

## Who Can Use This

- Lenders who have approved funding notice drawdowns

## When This Is Used

Use fund transfer confirmation when:
- You have approved a drawdown
- You have transferred funds to the borrower
- You want to mark the transfer as complete
- You need to finalize your participation

## Step-by-Step Process

### Transferring Funds

1. **Complete Transfer**
   - Transfer funds to borrower
   - Verify transfer was successful
   - Keep transfer confirmation details

2. **Access Confirmation**
   - Navigate to funding notice
   - Review your approval status
   - Access fund transfer confirmation

### Confirming Transfer

1. **Review Details**
   - Verify you've transferred funds
   - Check transfer amount matches allocation
   - Confirm transfer was successful

2. **Confirm Transfer**
   - Click confirm fund transfer button
   - Add confirmation note if needed
   - Submit confirmation
   - Status updates to show transfer confirmed

3. **After Confirmation**
   - Your participation is marked complete
   - Drawdown process finished for you
   - Status shows transfer confirmed
   - Process complete for your portion

## Rules & Validations

- You can only confirm after you've approved the drawdown
- You should transfer funds before confirming
- Confirmation marks your participation as complete
- Each lender confirms independently
- Your confirmation doesn't affect other lenders

## What Happens Next

After confirming fund transfer:
- Your participation is marked complete
- Drawdown process is finished for your portion
- Status shows transfer confirmed
- Borrower receives your funds
- Process complete for this drawdown

Understanding funds transfer confirmation helps you complete the drawdown process and finalize your participation in credit facility drawdowns.
