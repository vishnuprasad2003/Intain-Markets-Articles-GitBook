# Term Sheet Submission

## Overview

Term sheet submission is the process where borrowers submit their term sheets to facility agents for review. This guide covers how to prepare, sign, and submit term sheets effectively.

## Who Can Use This

- Borrowers who create term sheets

## When This Is Used

Use term sheet submission when:
- You've completed your term sheet
- You're ready to submit for review
- You want to propose a credit facility
- You need to move forward with facility setup

## Step-by-Step Process

### Preparing for Submission

1. **Complete Term Sheet**
   - Fill in all required fields
   - Specify facility terms
   - Upload required documents
   - Verify information is accurate

2. **Review Information**
   - Review all term sheet details
   - Verify amounts and rates
   - Check dates and terms
   - Ensure everything is complete

### Signing the Term Sheet

1. **Initiate Signing**
   - Click sign button
   - Electronic signature process begins
   - Review documents

2. **Complete Signing**
   - Review documents carefully
   - Apply your electronic signature
   - Complete all required signature fields
   - Finish signing process

3. **Verify Completion**
   - Status changes to show signed
   - You can preview signed version
   - Ready for submission

### Submitting the Term Sheet

1. **Preview Signed Version**
   - Review signed term sheet
   - Verify all information correct
   - Check documents are attached

2. **Submit for Review**
   - Click submit button
   - Confirm submission
   - Status changes to show under review

3. **Wait for Decision**
   - Facility agent reviews your term sheet
   - You receive notification of decision
   - Can be approved, rejected, or changes requested

## Rules & Validations

- You must sign the term sheet before submitting
- All required fields must be filled
- Required documents must be uploaded
- You can only submit once term sheet is signed
- Status must be signed before submission

## What Happens Next

After submitting term sheet:
- Facility agent reviews your proposal
- You receive notification of decision
- If approved, master commitment is automatically created
- If rejected, you can create a new term sheet
- If changes requested, you can update and resubmit

Understanding term sheet submission helps you effectively propose credit facilities and move through the approval process.
