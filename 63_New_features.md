## Key Concepts Explained

### Credit Facility Module Enhancements

Recent enhancements to credit facilities include:

- **Automatic Master Commitment Creation**: When term sheets are approved, master commitments are automatically created, streamlining the facility setup process.

- **Per-Lender E-Signature Tracking**: Facility agents can sign funding notices for each lender individually, with each lender's signature status tracked separately.

- **Token Generation and Distribution**: Enhanced token generation and distribution capabilities enable better tracking and management of drawdowns.

- **Individual Lender Approval Tracking**: Each lender's approval status is tracked individually, allowing for flexible lender participation.

- **Enhanced Status Workflows**: Improved status workflows provide better visibility and control throughout the facility lifecycle.

### Enhanced Status Tracking

Improvements to status tracking include:

- **Comprehensive Status History**: Complete status history shows all changes with who changed what and when.

- **Detailed Action Tracking**: All actions are tracked with complete details, providing full visibility.

- **Complete Audit Trails**: Enhanced audit trails ensure complete accountability and compliance.

- **Status Qualifiers**: Status qualifiers provide additional context about item states.

- **Enhanced Visibility**: Better visibility into status changes and workflow progression.

### Improved E-Signature Integration

E-signature improvements include:

- **Electronic Signature Support**: Full support for electronic signatures across all document types.

- **Per-Lender Signing**: Facility agents can sign funding notices separately for each lender.

- **Enhanced Signature Tracking**: Better tracking of signature completion and status.

- **Better Signature Visibility**: Improved visibility into who has signed and when.

- **Improved Workflow**: Enhanced workflow integration for signature processes.
