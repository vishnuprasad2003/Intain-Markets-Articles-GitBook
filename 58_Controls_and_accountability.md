# Controls and Accountability

## Overview

Intain Markets implements comprehensive controls and accountability measures to ensure security, compliance, and proper governance. Understanding these controls helps you understand how the platform protects all parties and maintains integrity.

## Who Can Use This

- All platform users

## When This Is Used

Understanding controls and accountability helps when:
- You want to understand platform security
- You need to know how access is controlled
- You want to understand accountability measures
- You need compliance information

## Step-by-Step Process

### Access Controls

1. **Role-Based Access**
   - Each role has specific permissions
   - Access limited to role capabilities
   - Cannot access unauthorized areas
   - Security maintained
   - Proper workflow enforced

2. **Authentication**
   - Users must authenticate to access
   - Secure login process
   - Session management
   - Access control
   - Security maintained

### Workflow Controls

1. **Status-Based Controls**
   - Status controls available actions
   - Cannot skip workflow steps
   - Proper sequence enforced
   - Workflow integrity maintained
   - Process protected

2. **Approval Gates**
   - Approvals required to proceed
   - Cannot skip approvals
   - Proper review ensured
   - Quality maintained
   - Risk controlled

### Data Controls

1. **Data Validation**
   - Data validated before acceptance
   - Format checks performed
   - Business rules enforced
   - Quality maintained
   - Errors prevented

2. **Audit Trails**
   - All actions tracked
   - Complete history maintained
   - Accountability ensured
   - Compliance supported
   - Transparency provided

## Rules & Validations

- Access is controlled by role and permissions
- Workflow steps cannot be skipped
- Approvals are required at appropriate stages
- All actions are tracked and auditable
- Data is validated before acceptance

## What Happens Next

After understanding controls and accountability:
- You understand platform security measures
- You know how access is controlled
- You understand accountability mechanisms
- You know how compliance is maintained
- You understand how integrity is protected

Understanding controls and accountability helps you appreciate how the platform ensures security, compliance, and proper governance for all parties.
