# Why Can't I Act

## Overview

This guide helps you understand why you can't take certain actions and how to resolve common issues preventing you from acting. Use this as a troubleshooting guide when actions are unavailable.

## Who Can Use This

- All platform users

## When This Is Used

Use this troubleshooting guide when:
- Actions are disabled or unavailable
- You can't proceed with your workflow
- You need to understand why actions aren't available
- You want to resolve blocking issues

## Step-by-Step Process

### Quick Diagnosis

1. **Check the Button**
   - Hover over disabled button - tooltip explains why
   - Check status message nearby
   - Look for error or warning messages
   - Review item status
   - Check requirements

2. **Check Your Role**
   - Verify you're logged in with correct role
   - Check if your role has permission for this action
   - Ensure you're assigned to this item
   - Verify you have access

3. **Check the Status**
   - Verify current status
   - Check if status allows this action
   - Ensure status is correct for workflow
   - Verify status hasn't changed unexpectedly

4. **Check Requirements**
   - Verify all required fields are filled
   - Check if documents are uploaded
   - Ensure prerequisites are met
   - Verify information is complete

### Common Scenarios

1. **Can't Submit Term Sheet**
   - Check status is signed
   - Verify electronic signature completed
   - Ensure all required fields filled
   - Check documents uploaded
   - Fix any validation errors

2. **Can't Create Funding Request**
   - Check master commitment is active
   - Verify facility is operational
   - Check available borrowing capacity
   - Ensure you have access to facility

3. **Can't Approve Item**
   - Check item is in review status
   - Verify you have permission to approve
   - Ensure you're the correct reviewer
   - Check all requirements are met

## Rules & Validations

- Actions are disabled for valid reasons
- Tooltips and messages explain why actions are disabled
- Status and permissions control availability
- Prerequisites must be met
- Workflow requirements must be satisfied

## What Happens Next

After troubleshooting:
- You understand why actions are unavailable
- You know what to do to enable actions
- You can resolve blocking issues
- You can proceed with your workflow
- You understand platform requirements

Understanding why you can't act helps you resolve issues and proceed with your workflow effectively.
