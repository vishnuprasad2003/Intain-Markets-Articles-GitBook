# Why Can't I Act

## Overview

This troubleshooting guide helps you understand why you can't take certain actions and how to resolve common issues preventing you from acting. Use this guide when actions are disabled or unavailable to diagnose and resolve problems.

## Applicable Users

- All platform users who encounter disabled actions or cannot proceed with workflows

## Rules Explained

### Quick Diagnosis Steps

Follow these steps to diagnose why actions are unavailable:

1. **Check the Button or Action**
   - Hover over disabled button - tooltip explains why it's disabled
   - Check status message nearby
   - Look for error or warning messages
   - Review item status badge
   - Check requirements section

2. **Check Your Role**
   - Verify you're logged in with correct role
   - Check if your role has permission for this action
   - Ensure you're assigned to this item
   - Verify you have access to this item
   - Confirm your role allows this action

3. **Check the Status**
   - Verify current status of the item
   - Check if status allows this action
   - Ensure status is correct for workflow stage
   - Verify status hasn't changed unexpectedly
   - Review status history if needed

4. **Check Requirements**
   - Verify all required fields are filled
   - Check if required documents are uploaded
   - Ensure prerequisites are met
   - Verify information is complete
   - Check validation messages

### Common Scenarios and Solutions

**Scenario 1: Can't Submit Term Sheet**

**Possible Causes**:
- Term sheet is not signed
- Electronic signature not completed
- Required fields not filled
- Required documents not uploaded
- Validation errors present

**Solutions**:
- Complete electronic signature
- Fill all required fields
- Upload all required documents
- Fix any validation errors
- Ensure term sheet is complete

**Scenario 2: Can't Create Funding Request**

**Possible Causes**:
- Master commitment is not active
- Facility is not operational
- No available borrowing capacity
- You don't have access to facility
- Facility status doesn't allow requests

**Solutions**:
- Wait for facility to become active
- Check facility status
- Verify available borrowing capacity
- Ensure you have access to facility
- Wait for facility activation

**Scenario 3: Can't Approve Item**

**Possible Causes**:
- Item is not in review status
- You don't have permission to approve
- You're not the correct reviewer
- Requirements not met
- Item already approved or rejected

**Solutions**:
- Verify item is in correct status for approval
- Check your role and permissions
- Ensure you're the assigned reviewer
- Verify all requirements are met
- Check if item was already decided

**Scenario 4: Can't Edit Item**

**Possible Causes**:
- Item is not in editable status
- Item is under review
- Item has been approved or finalized
- You don't have permission to edit
- Item is locked

**Solutions**:
- Check item status - must be Draft or Changes Requested
- Wait for review to complete if under review
- Cannot edit after approval or finalization
- Verify your role allows editing
- Check if item is locked by workflow

**Scenario 5: Can't Share Pool**

**Possible Causes**:
- Pool is not in Created or Preview status
- Required information incomplete
- Pool not ready for sharing
- Sharing already completed
- Status doesn't allow sharing

**Solutions**:
- Ensure pool is in Created or Preview status
- Complete all required information
- Prepare pool for sharing
- Check if already shared
- Verify status allows sharing

## Impact on Users

### When You Can't Act

- **Workflow Is Blocked**: You cannot proceed until issues are resolved.

- **Requirements Not Met**: Some condition prevents the action - identify and address it.

- **Need to Wait**: You may need to wait for others to act or for status to change.

- **Need to Complete Prerequisites**: You may need to complete required steps first.

- **Need to Verify Permissions**: You may need to verify your role and permissions.

### How to Resolve

- **Follow Diagnosis Steps**: Systematically check status, role, requirements, and permissions.

- **Address Issues**: Fix identified issues to enable actions.

- **Wait if Needed**: Wait for others to act or for status to change if required.

- **Complete Prerequisites**: Complete required steps before attempting actions.

- **Check Tooltips**: Hover over disabled buttons to see explanations.

## Important Notes

- **Actions are disabled for valid reasons** - Disabled actions prevent errors and ensure proper workflow.

- **Tooltips and messages explain why** - Hover over disabled buttons or check messages for explanations.

- **Status and permissions control availability** - Status and your role determine what actions are available.

- **Prerequisites must be met** - Required information, documents, or steps must be complete.

- **Workflow requirements must be satisfied** - All workflow requirements must be met before actions are enabled.

- **Systematic checking helps** - Check status, role, requirements, and permissions systematically.

- **Common issues are solvable** - Most blocking issues can be resolved by completing requirements or waiting for workflow progression.

- **Support is available** - Contact support if you cannot resolve blocking issues.

- **Documentation provides guidance** - Documentation explains requirements and workflow steps.

- **Patience may be required** - Some actions require waiting for workflow progression or other parties.

Understanding why you can't act helps you diagnose issues, resolve blocking problems, complete prerequisites, work effectively within workflow requirements, and proceed with your tasks once issues are resolved.
