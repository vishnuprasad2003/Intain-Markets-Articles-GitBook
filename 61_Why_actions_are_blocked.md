# Why Actions Are Blocked

## Overview

Understanding why actions are blocked helps you know what needs to happen before you can proceed. This guide explains common reasons actions are blocked and how to resolve them.

## Who Can Use This

- All platform users

## When This Is Used

Understanding blocked actions helps when:
- You see disabled buttons
- Actions aren't available
- You want to know why you can't proceed
- You need to understand requirements

## Step-by-Step Process

### Common Blocking Reasons

1. **Wrong Status**
   - Item isn't in the right status for the action
   - Workflow doesn't permit the action at current status
   - Must progress through statuses in order
   - Cannot skip workflow steps

2. **Missing Information**
   - Required fields not filled
   - Documents not uploaded
   - Information incomplete
   - Prerequisites missing

3. **Waiting for Others**
   - Another party needs to act first
   - Previous step not completed
   - Dependency not satisfied
   - Waiting for action

4. **No Permission**
   - Your role doesn't allow the action
   - You don't have access
   - Not assigned to item
   - Permissions not granted

5. **Already Completed**
   - Action already taken
   - Cannot repeat action
   - Already in final state
   - Process complete

### How to Resolve

1. **Check Status**
   - Verify current status
   - Complete required steps first
   - Progress through workflow properly
   - Wait for status to change

2. **Complete Information**
   - Fill all required fields
   - Upload required documents
   - Complete all information
   - Meet all prerequisites

3. **Wait for Others**
   - Wait for other party to act
   - Check what's pending
   - Monitor status changes
   - Follow up if needed

## Rules & Validations

- Actions are blocked for valid reasons
- Status controls what actions are available
- Prerequisites must be met before actions enabled
- Permissions determine what you can do
- Workflow progression ensures proper order

## What Happens Next

After understanding why actions are blocked:
- You know what needs to happen to enable actions
- You understand workflow requirements
- You can complete prerequisites
- You know when to wait for others
- You can resolve blocking issues

Understanding why actions are blocked helps you know what to do to enable actions and proceed with your workflow.
