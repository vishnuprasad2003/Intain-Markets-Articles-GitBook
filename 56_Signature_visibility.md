# Signature Visibility

## Overview

E-signatures are used throughout the platform for document signing. Understanding signature visibility helps you know who can see signatures, when they're visible, and how to access signed documents.

## Who Can Use This

- All users who sign documents
- All users who review signed documents

## When This Is Used

Understanding signature visibility helps when:
- You've signed a document
- You need to view signed documents
- You want to know who can see signatures
- You need to access signed versions

## Step-by-Step Process

### Where Signatures Are Used

1. **Term Sheets**
   - Borrower signs term sheet
   - Signature visible after completion
   - Signed document stored securely
   - Available to borrower and facility agent

2. **Master Commitments**
   - Lender signs master commitment
   - Signature visible after completion
   - Signed document stored securely
   - Available to lender and facility agent

3. **Funding Notices**
   - Facility agent signs for each lender
   - Individual signatures tracked separately
   - Signed documents stored securely
   - Available to relevant parties

### Accessing Signed Documents

1. **How to Access**
   - Navigate to item details page
   - Go to Documents section
   - Look for signed documents
   - Click to view or download
   - See signature details

2. **What You'll See**
   - Signed document version
   - Signature details
   - Signing timestamp
   - Signer information
   - Complete signed document

## Rules & Validations

- Signatures are visible after completion
- Signed documents are stored securely
- Authorized parties can view signatures
- Signatures are tracked with timestamps
- Complete audit trail is maintained

## What Happens Next

After understanding signature visibility:
- You know who can see signatures
- You understand when signatures are visible
- You can access signed documents
- You know how signatures are tracked
- You understand signature security

Understanding signature visibility helps you know how to access signed documents and understand who can see signatures throughout the platform.
