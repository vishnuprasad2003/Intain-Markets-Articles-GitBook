# Facility Approval

## Overview

Facility approval is the process where lenders review master commitments and approve them, activating the credit facility. This guide covers how lenders evaluate facilities and make approval decisions.

## Who Can Use This

- Lenders who receive master commitments for approval

## When This Is Used

Use facility approval when:
- Master commitments are submitted for your approval
- You need to evaluate facility proposals
- You want to activate credit facilities
- You're making facility participation decisions

## Step-by-Step Process

### Reviewing Master Commitments

1. **Access Master Commitment**
   - Check notifications or approval queue
   - Navigate to master commitments for approval
   - Open master commitment details
   - Review all information

2. **Evaluate Facility Structure**
   - Review facility terms and amounts
   - Assess interest rates and maturity
   - Evaluate facility rules and parameters
   - Review borrowing base configuration
   - Assess overall risk profile

3. **Review Lender Configuration**
   - Check your commitment amount
   - Review your participation percentage
   - Understand voting rights
   - Verify lender-specific terms

4. **Assess Facility Setup**
   - Evaluate facility setup completeness
   - Review calculation methods
   - Check rule definitions
   - Verify configuration quality

### Making Approval Decisions

1. **Approve Master Commitment**
   - Review thoroughly
   - Evaluate facility structure
   - Sign via electronic signature
   - Confirm approval
   - Facility becomes active

2. **Reject Master Commitment**
   - Review and identify issues
   - Click reject button
   - Provide rejection reason if needed
   - Confirm rejection

## Rules & Validations

- Any lender approval activates the facility
- You can only approve master commitments pending approval
- Electronic signature is required for approval
- Once approved, facility becomes active
- Your approval decision is tracked individually

## What Happens Next

After approving a master commitment:
- Facility becomes active and operational
- Borrowers can create funding requests
- Facility agent can manage active facility
- You can participate in funding
- Facility is fully functional

Understanding facility approval helps lenders effectively evaluate facilities and activate credit facilities for operation.
