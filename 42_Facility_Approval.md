# Facility Approval

## Overview

Facility approval is the process where lenders review master commitments and approve them, activating the credit facility. This guide covers how lenders evaluate facilities and make approval decisions.

## Receiving Master Commitments for Approval

### How Master Commitments Are Received

**When you receive a master commitment:**
- Facility agent submits master commitment
- Status changes to PendingLenderApproval
- You receive notification
- Master commitment appears in approval queue

### What You See

**Master commitment information:**
- Facility terms and structure
- Facility rules and parameters
- Borrowing base configuration
- Collateral eligibility rules
- Lender groups and participation
- Facility agent information

---

## Reviewing Master Commitments

### Step 1: Access Master Commitment

**How to access:**
1. Check notifications or approval queue
2. Navigate to master commitments for approval
3. Open master commitment details
4. Review all information

### Step 2: Evaluate Facility Structure

**What to evaluate:**
- Facility terms: Amount, rates, maturity
- Facility rules: Borrowing base, collateral rules
- Structure: How facility operates
- Risk profile: Overall risk assessment
- Terms: Acceptability of terms

### Step 3: Review Lender Configuration

**What to review:**
- Your commitment amount
- Your participation percentage
- Voting rights
- Lender-specific terms
- Other lender parameters

### Step 4: Assess Facility Setup

**What to assess:**
- Facility setup completeness
- Calculation methods
- Rule definitions
- Configuration quality
- Readiness for operation

---

## Making Approval Decisions

### Option 1: Approve Master Commitment

**When to approve:**
- Facility terms are acceptable
- Structure is appropriate
- Risk is acceptable
- Ready to proceed
- Terms meet your criteria

**How to approve:**
1. Review master commitment thoroughly
2. Evaluate facility structure
3. Initiate DocuSign signing
4. Sign master commitment electronically
5. Approval triggers status change

**What happens:**
- Master commitment status: ACTIVE
- Facility becomes operational
- Borrowers can create funding requests
- E-signature envelope generated (for documentation)
- Your lender status: approved or esignature_completed

**Key point:**
- Any lender approval activates facility
- One approval makes it ACTIVE
- Other lenders can still approve (for their own tracking)

### Option 2: Reject Master Commitment

**When to reject:**
- Facility terms not acceptable
- Structure not appropriate
- Risk too high
- Cannot proceed
- Terms don't meet criteria

**How to reject:**
1. Review master commitment
2. Decide to reject
3. Click "Reject" button (if available)
4. Provide rejection reason (if possible)
5. Confirm rejection

**What happens:**
- Your rejection recorded
- Facility may remain pending (other lenders can approve)
- Or facility may return to Draft (depends on system)
- Facility agent notified

## Approval Process

### DocuSign Signing

**Signing process:**
1. Review master commitment
2. Click "Approve" or "Sign"
3. DocuSign envelope created
4. Sign electronically
5. Approval completes

**After signing:**
- Status changes to ACTIVE
- Facility operational
- E-signature envelope generated
- Lender status updated

---

## Best Practices

### For Lenders

**Review process:**
1. **Review carefully**: Understand facility structure
2. **Evaluate terms**: Assess facility parameters
3. **Assess risk**: Evaluate risk profile
4. **Make informed decision**: Approve or reject based on evaluation
5. **Approve promptly**: Keep workflow moving

**Decision making:**
1. **Be thorough**: Review all aspects
2. **Be clear**: Understand what you're approving
3. **Be timely**: Don't delay unnecessarily
4. **Be professional**: Maintain good communication

1. Receive master commitment → Review
2. Terms acceptable → Approve
3. Sign via DocuSign → Approval
4. Status: ACTIVE → Facility operational

### Scenario 2: Careful Review

1. Receive master commitment → Review
2. Evaluate thoroughly → Assess terms
3. Review structure → Verify setup
4. Approve when satisfied → ACTIVE

### Scenario 3: Rejection

1. Receive master commitment → Review
2. Terms not acceptable → Reject
3. Provide feedback → Facility agent informed

## Troubleshooting

### "Can't see master commitment"
- Check notifications
- Verify status is PendingLenderApproval
- Check approval queue
- Ensure you're a lender in facility

### "Can't approve"
- Check master commitment status
- Verify you're a lender
- Ensure DocuSign available
- Check if already approved

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Review thoroughly**: Understand facility before approving
2. **Any lender activates**: One approval makes facility ACTIVE
3. **E-signature required**: Must sign via DocuSign
4. **Individual tracking**: Each lender's approval tracked separately
5. **Facility operational**: ACTIVE status enables funding requests

Understanding facility approval helps lenders effectively evaluate master commitments and make informed decisions about participating in credit facilities.
