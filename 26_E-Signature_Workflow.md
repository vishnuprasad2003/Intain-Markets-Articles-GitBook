# E-Signature Workflow

## Overview

E-signatures are used throughout the credit facility workflow to digitally sign important documents. The platform supports electronic signatures, enabling a complete digital workflow without paper documents.

## Who Can Use This

- Borrowers who sign term sheets
- Facility Agents who sign funding notices
- Lenders who sign master commitments

## When This Is Used

Use e-signatures when:
- You need to sign term sheets
- You need to sign master commitments
- You need to sign funding notices
- You want to complete documents digitally

## Step-by-Step Process

### Signing Term Sheets

1. **Initiate Signing**
   - Borrower initiates electronic signature
   - Documents are prepared for signing
   - Signing interface opens

2. **Review and Sign**
   - Review documents carefully
   - Apply your electronic signature
   - Complete all required signature fields

3. **Completion**
   - Signing is completed
   - Status updates to show signed
   - Term sheet is ready for submission

### Signing Master Commitments

1. **Review Facility**
   - Lender reviews master commitment
   - Evaluates facility terms
   - Decides to approve

2. **Sign and Approve**
   - Lender signs via electronic signature
   - Facility becomes active when approved
   - Status updates to show active

### Signing Funding Notices

1. **Facility Agent Signs**
   - Facility agent signs for each lender
   - Individual signing per lender
   - Each lender's signature tracked separately

2. **Completion**
   - All required signatures completed
   - Funding notice ready for borrower approval
   - Process can proceed

## Rules & Validations

- Electronic signatures are legally binding
- You must sign before documents can proceed
- Some documents require multiple signatures
- Signatures are tracked with timestamps
- Signed documents are stored securely

## What Happens Next

After completing e-signatures:
- Documents proceed to next stage
- Status updates reflect signing completion
- Workflow continues based on document type
- Signed documents are stored securely
- Complete audit trail is maintained

Understanding e-signature workflow helps you complete documents digitally and move through the credit facility process efficiently.
