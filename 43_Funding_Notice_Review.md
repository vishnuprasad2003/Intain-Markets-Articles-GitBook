# Funding Notice Review

## Overview

Funding notice review is the process where lenders review funding notices after borrower token approval and decide whether to approve or reject individual drawdowns. This guide covers how lenders evaluate funding notices and make approval decisions.

## Who Can Use This

- Lenders who receive funding notices

## When This Is Used

Use funding notice review when:
- Funding notices become visible after borrower approval
- You need to review drawdown requests
- You want to approve or reject participation
- You're making drawdown decisions

## Step-by-Step Process

### Reviewing Funding Notices

1. **Access Funding Notice**
   - Check notifications or review queue
   - Navigate to funding notices
   - Open funding notice details
   - Review all information

2. **Evaluate Drawdown Request**
   - Review request amount
   - Check purpose of funds
   - Verify funding date
   - Assess borrowing base impact
   - Evaluate risk factors

3. **Review Token Allocation**
   - Check your allocated token amount
   - Verify your participation percentage
   - Review total drawdown amount
   - Confirm distribution details

4. **Assess Facility Impact**
   - Evaluate borrowing base impact
   - Check utilization after drawdown
   - Review remaining capacity
   - Assess facility health

### Making Approval Decisions

1. **Approve Drawdown**
   - Review thoroughly
   - Evaluate drawdown request
   - Click approve button
   - Confirm approval
   - Your participation is confirmed

2. **Reject Drawdown**
   - Review and identify concerns
   - Click reject button
   - Provide rejection reason if needed
   - Confirm rejection
   - Your portion will not be funded

## Rules & Validations

- Each lender makes independent decision
- Your decision doesn't affect other lenders
- You can approve or reject based on your evaluation
- Approved lenders can proceed with fund transfer
- Rejected lenders don't participate in that drawdown

## What Happens Next

After making your decision:
- If approved, you can proceed with fund transfer
- If rejected, you don't participate in this drawdown
- Other lenders make their own decisions independently
- Borrower can see which lenders approved
- Fund transfer process proceeds for approved lenders

Understanding funding notice review helps lenders make informed decisions on drawdowns and participate effectively in credit facilities.
