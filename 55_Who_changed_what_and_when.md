# Who Changed What and When

## Overview

The platform tracks every change with complete details about who made the change, what changed, and when it happened. This guide explains how to view and understand this change history.

## Who Can Use This

- All platform users

## When This Is Used

Understanding change history helps when:
- You need to see who made changes
- You want to know when changes occurred
- You need to track item progression
- You want to understand process flow
- You need audit information

## Step-by-Step Process

### Viewing Status History

1. **Access Status History**
   - Navigate to item details page
   - Go to Status History section
   - View chronological list of changes
   - See who changed what and when

2. **Understanding Status History**
   - Each entry shows status change
   - Displays who made the change
   - Shows when change occurred
   - May include reason for change
   - Shows previous and new status

### Viewing Action History

1. **Access Action History**
   - Navigate to item details page
   - Go to Action History section
   - View chronological list of actions
   - See who took what action and when

2. **Understanding Action History**
   - Each entry shows action taken
   - Displays who took the action
   - Shows when action occurred
   - Includes what was affected
   - Shows result of action

### Viewing Document History

1. **Access Document History**
   - Navigate to item details page
   - Go to Documents section
   - View document history
   - See uploads and changes

2. **Understanding Document History**
   - Shows document uploads
   - Displays who uploaded documents
   - Shows when documents were uploaded
   - Tracks document changes
   - Maintains version history

## Rules & Validations

- All changes are tracked automatically
- History cannot be modified or deleted
- Complete information is recorded
- Timestamps are accurate
- User information is included for all changes

## What Happens Next

After understanding change history:
- You can track who made changes
- You know when changes occurred
- You can review item progression
- You understand process flow
- You have complete audit information

Understanding who changed what and when helps you track item history, maintain accountability, and understand the complete progression of any item in the platform.
