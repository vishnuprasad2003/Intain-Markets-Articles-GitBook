# Pool Sharing and Mandates

## Overview

Sharing pools allows you to collaborate with other organizations in the structured finance process. You can share pools with market makers, investors, rating agencies, and other parties who need to review or participate in your transaction.

## Who Can Use This

- Issuers who create pools and want to share them
- Market Makers who receive pool shares and mandates
- Investors who receive pool previews
- Rating Agencies and other parties involved in transactions

## When This Is Used

Use pool sharing when you:
- Want market makers to review and accept mandates
- Need investors to see investment opportunities
- Want rating agencies to analyze pools
- Need to collaborate with other parties

## Step-by-Step Process

### Sharing a Pool

1. **Prepare Your Pool**
   - Ensure pool information is complete
   - Map loans to the pool
   - Review metrics for accuracy
   - Verify all details are correct

2. **Select Recipients**
   - Choose which organizations to share with
   - Select market makers, investors, rating agencies, or other parties
   - Assign organizations to the pool

3. **Set Sharing Permissions**
   - Control what recipients can see and do
   - Allow or restrict feedback and downloads
   - Set appropriate access levels

4. **Share the Pool**
   - Complete the sharing process
   - Recipients receive notifications
   - Pool becomes visible to shared parties

### Managing Mandates

1. **Submit Pool for Mandate**
   - Submit pool to market maker for review
   - Market maker sees the pool and reviews it
   - Status changes to reflect mandate review

2. **Market Maker Reviews**
   - Market maker analyzes pool details and metrics
   - Market maker decides whether to accept
   - Market maker can request changes or provide feedback

3. **Market Maker Decision**
   - Accept: Market maker accepts the mandate and structures the deal
   - Reject: Market maker declines, pool returns to available status
   - Request Changes: Market maker provides feedback for improvements

4. **Mandate Accepted**
   - Pool moves forward with the market maker
   - Market maker proceeds with deal structuring
   - Pool progresses toward final deal

## Rules & Validations

- You can share pools while they're in Created or Preview status
- Sharing permissions control what recipients can do
- A pool can be shared with multiple parties simultaneously
- Market makers can accept or reject mandates
- Mandate acceptance moves pool toward deal status
- You can share with different market makers if one rejects

## What Happens Next

After sharing a pool:
- Recipients can view and analyze the pool
- Market makers can accept mandates to structure deals
- Investors can review and express interest
- Rating agencies can analyze pool characteristics
- You receive feedback and can respond accordingly

Pool sharing enables collaboration with other parties, helping you move pools through the structured finance workflow and complete successful transactions.
