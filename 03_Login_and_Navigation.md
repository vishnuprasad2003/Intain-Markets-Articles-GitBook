# Login and Navigation

## Overview

Access Intain Markets by logging in with your credentials and role. The platform provides a role-based interface that shows you relevant features and information based on your role.

## Who Can Use This

- All platform users with valid accounts

## When This Is Used

Use login and navigation when you:
- First access the platform
- Return to the platform after a session expires
- Need to switch between different sections
- Want to find specific items or features

## Step-by-Step Process

### Logging In

1. **Access the Login Page**
   - Open your web browser and go to the Intain Markets login page
   - You'll see a login form with fields for Email, Password, and Role

2. **Enter Your Credentials**
   - Email: Enter your registered email address
   - Password: Enter your password
   - Role: Select your role from the dropdown

3. **Submit Login**
   - Click the "Login" button
   - If successful, you'll be redirected to your role-based dashboard
   - If there's an error, check your credentials and try again

### Navigating the Platform

1. **View Your Dashboard**
   - After logging in, you'll see your dashboard
   - Review pending actions requiring your attention
   - Check recent activity and notifications

2. **Use the Main Menu**
   - Click menu items to access different sections
   - Menu options vary based on your role
   - Use breadcrumbs to see your current location

3. **Find Specific Items**
   - Use search to find items by name or ID
   - Apply filters to narrow down lists
   - Sort lists by date, name, or status

4. **Take Actions**
   - Click on items to view details
   - Use action buttons to perform tasks
   - Enabled buttons indicate available actions
   - Disabled buttons show reasons why actions aren't available

## Rules & Validations

- You must enter a valid email address registered in the system
- Your password must match your account password
- You must select the correct role that matches your account
- Your account must be active to log in successfully
- Sessions expire after a period of inactivity

## What Happens Next

After logging in:
- You'll see your dashboard with pending actions and recent activity
- You can navigate to different sections using the main menu
- You can view items relevant to your role
- You can take actions based on your permissions
- You'll receive notifications about status changes

If your session expires, you'll need to log in again. The platform remembers your preferences and shows you the most relevant information for your role.
