# Why Approvals Exist

## Overview

Approvals are a fundamental part of the Intain Markets platform. Understanding why approvals exist helps you understand the workflow and know what to expect at each stage.

## Who Can Use This

- All platform users

## When This Is Used

Understanding approvals helps when:
- You're waiting for approval on your submission
- You need to approve something as a reviewer
- You want to understand the workflow
- You need to know why certain actions require approval

## Step-by-Step Process

### Purpose of Approvals

1. **Quality Assurance**
   - Items are reviewed before proceeding
   - Standards are maintained
   - Quality is verified
   - Issues are caught early

2. **Compliance**
   - Regulatory requirements met
   - Business rules followed
   - Policies enforced
   - Standards maintained

3. **Risk Management**
   - Risk is assessed before commitment
   - Problems identified early
   - Risk is controlled
   - Decisions are informed

4. **Transparency**
   - Clear decision points
   - Visible review process
   - Documented decisions
   - Audit trail maintained

### Types of Approvals

1. **Single Approval**
   - One party approves
   - Workflow proceeds immediately
   - Simple approval mechanism

2. **Multi-Party Approval**
   - Multiple parties must approve
   - Can be sequential or parallel
   - More complex approval process

3. **Sequential Approval**
   - Approvals happen in order
   - Each approval unlocks next step
   - Ensures proper workflow progression

## Rules & Validations

- Approvals ensure quality and compliance
- They act as gates in the workflow
- Some approvals require specific roles
- Approvals can be one-way or allow changes
- Approval history is maintained for audit

## What Happens Next

After understanding approvals:
- You know why approvals are required
- You understand the approval process
- You know what to expect when waiting for approval
- You understand your role in approvals
- You can navigate workflows more effectively

Understanding why approvals exist helps you appreciate the workflow structure and know what to expect at each approval stage.
