# Term Sheet Review

## Overview

Term sheet review is the process where facility agents evaluate term sheets submitted by borrowers and decide whether to approve, reject, or request changes. This guide covers how facility agents review term sheets effectively.

## Who Can Use This

- Facility Agents who review term sheets

## When This Is Used

Use term sheet review when:
- Borrowers submit term sheets for review
- You need to evaluate facility proposals
- You want to approve or reject term sheets
- You need to ensure compliance with requirements

## Step-by-Step Process

### Reviewing Term Sheets

1. **Access Term Sheet**
   - Check notifications or review queue
   - Navigate to term sheets for review
   - Open term sheet details
   - Review all information

2. **Evaluate Terms**
   - Assess facility amount appropriateness
   - Evaluate interest rates and terms
   - Review facility structure
   - Assess borrower creditworthiness

3. **Review Documentation**
   - Check financial statements completeness
   - Verify required documents are included
   - Review supporting materials
   - Assess documentation quality

4. **Assess Compliance**
   - Verify terms meet facility requirements
   - Check borrower qualifications
   - Ensure documentation is complete
   - Confirm compliance with policies

### Making Review Decisions

1. **Approve Term Sheet**
   - Review thoroughly
   - Verify all requirements met
   - Click approve button
   - Confirm approval
   - Master commitment is automatically created

2. **Reject Term Sheet**
   - Review and identify issues
   - Click reject button
   - Provide rejection reason
   - Confirm rejection
   - Borrower can create new term sheet

3. **Request Changes**
   - Identify needed modifications
   - Click request changes button
   - Provide change details
   - Confirm change request
   - Borrower can update and resubmit

## Rules & Validations

- You can approve, reject, or request changes
- Approved term sheets automatically create master commitments
- Rejected term sheets cannot be resubmitted
- Change requests allow borrowers to update and resubmit
- Your decision affects facility setup workflow

## What Happens Next

After reviewing a term sheet:
- If approved, master commitment is automatically created
- If rejected, borrower receives reason and can create new term sheet
- If changes requested, borrower can update and resubmit
- Status updates reflect your decision
- Borrower is notified of the outcome

Understanding term sheet review helps facility agents effectively evaluate proposals and ensure facility requirements are met.
